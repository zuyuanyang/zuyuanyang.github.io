About Me


I obtained my Ph.D degree from the South China University of Technology in 2009, and visited Aizu university, Japan after graduation. I have been an Alfred Post-doctor in Deakin University, Australia. Currently, I am a Professor at School of Automation, Guangdong University of Technology, working closely with Profs. Shengli Xie, Shuxue Ding and Yong Xiang.

I have got Natural Science Funds of China for Excellent Young Scholars(国家优秀青年科学基金), Guangdong Natural Science Funds for Distinguished Young Scholars(广东省自然科学杰出青年基金), and joined the Program for New Century Excellent Talents in University of Ministry of Education of China(教育部新世纪优秀人才支持计划).
